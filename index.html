About us

Let Us Tell You Our Story

We empower diverse voices and stories all over the world, helping you build a community of readers. We champion untold stories to the publishing & entertainment industries. When you share your story, you’ll find a community who shares your unique worldview.

What you should do

Everyone has a story

Write the exact story you love to read, and someone else will love it too. Create lasting impact with your readers. When you share your story on our platform, your seekers escape with you into the world you’ve built, become invested in your storyline, and connect to you and your story like never before.

Post Often

Update regularly to encourage buzz and anticipation from a newly engaged audience, plus get ratings and reviews as you write.

Get Featured

As story seekers connect to your story that projects lasting impact, you can be get featured in their all read stories list.

Move Us

Let us tell stories that move us to the core, because when our pen is trembling, the seeker can feel it, too.

Follow Your Passion

Tell a story about something you are passionate about. Your passion will carry you through the ups and downs of the process.
